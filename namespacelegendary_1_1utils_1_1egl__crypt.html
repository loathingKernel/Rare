Detailed Description

Stripped down version of https://github.com/boppreh/aes which is in turn based on https://github.com/bozhu/AES-Python with ECB decryption added.

You should practically never roll your own crypto like this. In this case it's just unimportant enough since all it needs to do is decrypt some data from the EGL config file.
