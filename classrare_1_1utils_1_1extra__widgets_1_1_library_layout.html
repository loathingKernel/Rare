Arranges the widgets for this layout. 

        Layout
        +-----------------------------------------------+
        | margin (above first row only)                 |
        |-----------------------------------------------|
        | vspace (hspace)                               |
        |-----------------------------------------------|
        | hpadding                                      |
        |-----------------------------------------------|
        | _  _  __ +--------+ _  __ +--------+ _  __  _ |
        ||m||h|| h|| Widget ||h|| h|| Widget ||h|| h||m||
        ||a||s|| p||        ||s|| p||        ||s|| p||a||
        ||r||p|| a||        ||p|| a||        ||p|| a||r||
        ||g||a|| d||        ||a|| d||        ||a|| d||g||
        ||i||c|| d||        ||c|| d||        ||c|| d||i||
        ||n||e|| i||        ||e|| i||        ||e|| i||n||
        || || || n||        || || n||        || || n|| ||
        || || || g||        || || g||        || || g|| ||
        | -  -  -- +--------+ -  -- +--------+ -  --  - |
        |-----------------------------------------------|
        | vspace (hspace)                               |
        |-----------------------------------------------|
        | hpadding                                      |
        |-----------------------------------------------|
        | margin (below last row only)                  |
        +-----------------------------------------------+

        margin:   doesn't play a role in the code below, it only affects the
                  effective rectangle inside which we can layout
        hspace:   static padding between widgets (minimum distance between them)
        hpadding: dynamic padding to fill the space when resizing
Parameters
selfobject self-reference
rectthe area the widgets should occupy
testonlyonly test the layout, don't arrange the items
Returns
: the height of the layout
